A private player for your legal media sources.

OwnSource Player is a native iPhone and iPad app for user-provided M3U playlists, XMLTV guides, and provider logins. The app does not include channels, subscriptions, providers, or copyrighted media.

Privacy Policy Get Support
  • Native SwiftUI interface for iPhone and iPad
  • AVPlayer playback with AirPlay and Picture in Picture support
  • Local-first library, favorites, parental controls, and guide data

Bring Your Own Source

Add legal M3U URLs, local playlist files, XMLTV guides, or provider credentials that you already have permission to use.

No Bundled Content

OwnSource Player does not sell, recommend, bundle, or provide TV channels, movies, sports, providers, playlists, or subscriptions.

Local By Default

Your library, favorites, recent items, guide data, and parental settings are stored on your device. Provider passwords and parental PINs use Keychain.

App Store review note: This product is a media player only. Screenshots, demo data, and marketing must avoid copyrighted channels, premium providers, sports, movies, or claims of free TV access.
