Before Contacting Support

  • Confirm that your source URL starts with http:// or https://.
  • Confirm that you have permission to use the playlist, stream, guide, or provider account.
  • Try refreshing the source from the Sources screen.
  • Do not send provider passwords, private playlist URLs, or copyrighted media files in support requests.

What The App Does Not Provide

OwnSource Player does not provide channels, playlists, subscriptions, premium TV, sports access, movies, providers, or recommendations for third-party media services.
