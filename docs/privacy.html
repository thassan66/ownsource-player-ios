Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Overview

OwnSource Player is a media player for legal sources provided by the user. The app does not include an app-operated account system, bundled media, channel lists, subscriptions, provider marketplace, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or developer-operated media service.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores the sources you add, imported channel and media lists, favorites, recently watched items, resume positions, parental category settings, and imported XMLTV guide data locally on your device. Provider passwords and parental control PINs are stored in the device Keychain.

Network Requests

The app contacts only the playlist URLs, guide URLs, and provider servers that you enter. Those third-party services may receive your IP address, request details, and any credentials you choose to submit to them. Their handling of that information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Information We Collect

The current app does not transmit your playlists, provider credentials, watch history, favorites, parental settings, or guide data to Gloud Tech Solutions. We do not use in-app analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party crash reporting in the current app.

Support Requests

If you contact support by email or another external channel, we may receive the contact details and message content you choose to provide. Do not send provider passwords, playlist credentials, or private media URLs in support requests.

No Sale Or Tracking

We do not sell personal information. The current app does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising or advertising measurement.

Your Choices

You can remove saved sources, imported library data, guide data, favorites, parental settings, and stored provider credentials from the app by using Clear All Local Data in Settings. You can also delete the app from your device to remove app-local data managed by iOS.

Children

OwnSource Player is not directed to children. Parental controls are local convenience features and are not a substitute for device-level restrictions or supervision.

Changes

If the app later adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, or other services that change data handling, this policy and App Store privacy answers should be updated before release.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support details listed on the Support page.

OwnSource Player is a media player only and does not provide channels, playlists, providers, or subscriptions.
